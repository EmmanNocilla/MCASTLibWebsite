Circulation Services

Borrowing Made Easy

Our circulation services handle user registration, borrowing, returns, renewals, and queries. Get started by registering with the library to access all our resources.

Borrowing Entitlements

User Type Items Allowed
Levels 1-3 Students 4 items
Levels 4-8 Students 5 items
Academic/Administrative Staff 6 items

Loan Periods

Item Type Loan Period
Books 3 Weeks
A-V Material (Subject) 1 Week
A-V Material (Films - staff only) 3 Days
Periodicals 1 Week
Games & Toys 1 Week
Dissertations On-premises only

Renewals & Returns

Items can be renewed twice online through My Library Account, or in person at any circulation desk. Returns can be made to any MCAST library location.

Printing, Copying & Scanning

Photocopying & Printing Costs

Service A4 A3
Black & White €0.05 €0.10
Color €0.25 €0.50

Color printing available at Main Campus (Paola) and Institute for Creative Arts (Mosta) only.

Card System

Photocopying uses a card-operated system:

  • New Card: €3.00 (€2.50 deposit + €0.50 credit)
  • Recharge: €2.50 minimum
  • Deposit Refund: Available when card is returned

Scanning & Binding

Scanning: Free of charge, available through photocopier stations

Binding: Plastic spiral binding available at Mosta campus (€1.00 up to 50 pages, €1.50 for more)

Additional Services

Library Instruction & Tours

We offer orientation sessions, library tours, and training on using our resources effectively. Sessions are available throughout the year for individuals and groups.

Email us to schedule a session

Computing Facilities

All libraries provide computer access with internet connectivity for research, assignments, and email. Login details are provided by the IT department.

Support for Students with Difficulties

We're committed to ensuring all students can access our services:

  • Extended loan periods available
  • Fetching service for library materials
  • Individual library tours and training
  • Accessible study spaces
  • Read&Write assistive software
  • Wheelchair accessible facilities

Contact us at llrc@mcast.edu.mt to discuss your specific needs.
