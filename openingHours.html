Library Opening Hours

Find opening times and contact information for all MCAST library locations across Malta and Gozo.

Important Notice

Please note the following general conditions:

  • The Main Library in Paola operates Monday through Friday, with regular hours from 7:15 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. The library offers extended hours from October to May, remaining open until 7:30 p.m.
  • During the summer break (July to mid-September) and certain holiday periods, the library's hours may be adjusted, closing at 1:30 p.m.
  • The main and offsite libraries are closed on weekends, public holidays, and during the MCAST Christmas shutdown.
  • Offsite libraries are also closed during the Summer term recesses.
  • Offsite libraries will be closed on days when the designated staff member is on leave. We recommend that visitors from outside the respective campuses call to confirm the library's availability before planning their visit.
