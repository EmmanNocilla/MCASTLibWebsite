Suggest New Resources

Is there a particular book, journal, database, etc. we don't have that would make a useful addition to the library? Do you think we need more copies of an important textbook?

The MCAST library aims to make books and other resources available to support your learning and research. If the library does not have the material you require, you can suggest that we purchase it.

Request Status Tracking

You can follow the status of your request by logging into your online My Library Account and clicking on the Purchase requests button.

Making a Suggestion

Suggestions can be made by both MCAST staff and students.

Before submitting a suggestion, please use the online library catalogue to make sure that the library does not already have the item. For guidelines on how to do the search, please see Search Tips.

How to Suggest a Purchase

1

Check the Catalogue

Search our online catalogue to ensure we don't already have the item you're requesting.

2

Choose Your Method

Use the online form for books, or email us for other types of materials.

3

Submit Your Suggestion

Fill out the appropriate form with all necessary details about the resource.

4

Track Your Request

Monitor the status of your suggestion through your My Library Account.

What Happens Next?

We will consider all suggestions, bearing in mind their suitability as well as any budget constraints that may be in place. Each suggestion is evaluated based on:

  • Relevance to MCAST curriculum and research
  • Current collection gaps
  • Available budget
  • Demand and potential usage
