Quick Search Tips

Improve Your Search Results

  • Use quotation marks for exact phrases: "renewable energy"
  • Use AND to combine terms: solar AND energy
  • Use OR to expand your search: university OR college
  • Use NOT to exclude terms: energy NOT nuclear
  • Use an asterisk (*) for word variations: comput* finds computer, computing, computation
  • Use parentheses to group terms: (climate OR weather) AND change
  • Check spelling if you get few or no results
  • Try synonyms if your search terms aren't working
